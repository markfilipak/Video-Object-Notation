Streams —— visualization of the physical properties of an 'NTSC' video for example, having only 7 frames.
There are more structures and headers than are shown here but what's here is what matters most. There are 4 pixels (2x2) per quad, 8 quads in a 16x2 pair of rows, 16 rows per macroblock, 45 macroblocks per slice, 30 slices per 'picture'. The 'pictures' are encoded, muxed in with audio and subtitles, and then packetized.
